Boston Ruby Group

Boston Ruby Group helps you make connections, hack on projects, learn new things, and have fun with other Ruby enthusiasts.

The Boston Ruby Group is a community of Ruby language enthusiasts. We hold monthly meetings on the second Tuesday of the month with two long-form talks. We also have a project night on the first Monday of the month.

  • Project Nights

  • Meetings

    Second Tuesday of every month

    Listen to two long-form talks about something cool created with Ruby.
